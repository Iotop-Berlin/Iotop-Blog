About Us

This is what I do.

Who are we, and what do we do? We are Iotop (=iotop) a team of 3 and we are currently working on a solution to a problem related to plants. The problem with plants is that most people don't know how to take care of them. There are already solutions available, but they have a big problem: UX. They connect via Bluetooth to sensors which adds a barrier between users and their plants.

We want to solve this problem by connecting the sensor to the wifi so it sends data all the time and the user can retrieve it with a mobile app all the time, even when not at home, so that they can take care of their plants.
